About this Interface

Quan-Taara Interface Mark-I is an interactive physics and astrophysics compendium. The background renders a procedurally generated pixelated black hole — complete with a rotating accretion disk exhibiting Keplerian differential rotation, relativistic polar jets, and a gravitational lensing halo — all rendered at 1/4 resolution for the pixel-art aesthetic. Click any discipline card to explore its equations and derivations. Ask the AI interface any physics question.
